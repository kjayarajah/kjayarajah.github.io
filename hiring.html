Open Positions

I have openings for multiple PhD students for working with me on exciting projects on human-autonomy teaming and mobile/pervasive computing.

Sample Projects

  • Attention-sharing Human-Drone Teams [vision, demo]
  • Non-verbal Interactive Communication for Human-Robot teams [demo]
  • Collaborative Intelligence for the Internet of Things [vision, paper]
  • Human Behavior-aware Smart Environments [paper, paper, paper]

Experience in one or more of the following will be ideal:

  • Programming experience with collecting and processing sensor data (e.g., IoT sensors for smarthome settings, RGB/Lidar data processing, smartphone/smart watch-based sensing)
  • Programming experience with AR/VR platforms (e.g., MS HoloLens/C/Unity/DirectX)
  • Programming experience with edge platforms (e.g., Raspberry Pi, Jetson Nano/Xavier)
  • Programming experience with robotic platforms (e.g., ground robots, drones, ROS)
  • Familiarity with perception models (e.g., object detection/segmentation) and/or autonomous navigation
  • Publishing at local/international conferences

Contact Me. If you're interested, drop me an email at kasthuri@umbc.edu with a short summary (3-4 sentences) of your background and your CV.

How to Apply. For general guidance on the NJIT CS PhD/Masters programme and admission process, pls. see here and here.

Masters/undergraduate students at NJIT looking for projects on the above broad topics for their indepedent studies are most welcome to reach out to me!
